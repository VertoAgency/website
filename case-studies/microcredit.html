The company

About Microcredit.

Microcredit is a leading microfinance provider offering small-ticket consumer and SME lending across regulated European markets. The product sits inside the microfinance and consumer lending category — alongside other regional non-bank lenders and digital lending platforms — and competes on its accessible underwriting and its strength in serving consumers underserved by traditional banks.

The customer is a credit-seeking individual or small business across the markets the company serves. Compliance with consumer protection law and data privacy regulation is a core operating constraint — making analytics infrastructure and consent management strategic, not just technical.

The problem

The problem.

Microcredit needed to upgrade its analytics infrastructure to comply with EU regulations including GDPR. They required migration from Universal Analytics to GA4, integration of Google Consent Mode v2, and a custom solution for enhanced conversion tracking within their multi-step application form. The stakes were higher than typical: regulatory non-compliance carries real legal risk in their sector.

The work

What we built.

Our team conducted a thorough audit of Microcredit's existing setup and developed a clear roadmap for migrating to GA4. Working with the client's development team, we ensured seamless integration of Cookiebot and created a custom dataLayer for accurate funnel tracking. We also implemented Google Consent Mode v2 and updated Google Ads Enhanced Conversions with a custom script. We were instrumental in consulting, implementing, and validating the entire analytics transformation.
