Why VertoDigital exists

Most B2B funnels look healthy at the top and break in the middle.

VertoDigital was founded in Sofia in 2018 by Zoran Arsovski — built around a single observation. CMOs were walking into board meetings with MQL counts, while CFOs were asking the only question that mattered: where's the pipeline? The two numbers rarely matched. Most agencies didn't seem to notice.

So we built one that did. We narrowed the practice to B2B technology — long sales cycles, multi-stakeholder buying committees, ARR that has to be defended quarter after quarter. The work isn't easier there. It's just measurable. A click is a click. A pipeline-stage opportunity, attached to a deal, weighted by close probability — that's a number you can stake a forecast on.

Eight years later, the team is 50+ people across Sofia and Boston. The clients changed. The mission didn't. We still optimize for the only metric that survives a board review: pipeline that closes.

— Zoran Arsovski, Managing Partner

How we work

Three standards. No negotiation.

These aren't suggestions. They're what the work is held to — in the brief, in the deck, and in the Slack thread at 11pm.

01

Pipeline, not vanity metrics.

You report on what moves pipeline, not what fills a slide. If a metric doesn't connect to an opportunity stage, it doesn't go in the report. If a number can't survive a CFO's question, it doesn't go in the deck.

02

Data over opinion. Always.

Recommendations are backed by data, not instinct. We benchmark against 45+ accounts before forming a view. "I think" is the start of a conversation — not a conclusion. The data decides.

03

Specialists, not generalists.

We work in B2B technology and nothing else. Every client makes us sharper for the next. That specialism — 200+ companies, same buyer profile — is the thing that's genuinely hard to replicate.
